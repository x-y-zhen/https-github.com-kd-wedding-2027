David and Katherine cordially invite you to celebrate their union.

This celebration will be held at historic Josephine Butler Parks Center, overlooking Meridian Hill and Malcolm X Park. Josephine Butler organized the first union of black women laundry workers in the country, helped to desegregate the schools in Adams Morgan, and co-founded the D.C. Statehood Party as a means for advancing civil rights and black power in a majority-black city controlled by the U.S. Congress. We are excited to host our wedding celebration at this historic mansion named after an activist and leader.
